Who We Are

Not simply a football team

Perfect Touch Football Club is a Nigerian-based football development academy and FIFA-linked player agency focused on identifying, training, mentoring, and transferring talented athletes into professional football environments worldwide.

We are a football development academy, a player recruitment pipeline, a professional scouting organization, and a gateway connecting African football talent to international opportunities — built on three decades of discipline, technical mastery, and genuine pathways.

01

Our Mission

To provide African footballers with world-class development opportunities and pathways into professional football.

02

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's most respected football development and player placement organizations.

03

Our Standard

Elite coaching, athlete welfare, and a scouting network reaching clubs and agents across Europe, Asia, and Africa.
